On a Faithful Day, sometime in 2016, I decided to move to Osogbo to join my Father in the Lord, Pastor Joe Agbaje. I was asked to meet up with the PETRA FAMILY at Ekiti where a ministry event was been covered by the team. When I got there, I saw for the first time a dedicated and focused young Lady, Passionate about what she does. As time went by, we became friends. She was the welfare person at that time and was always ready to listen. Years after we pathed ways, our friendship eventually grew stonger and we realized our vision was connected. Today, we are together despite all the challenges. God has #MADE us better and Stronger and we know nothing less but #Grace. #Made21 is #GraceDay because #GraceIsOurFather.
